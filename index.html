What is Macramé?


Macramé is an ancient art that has roots that trace back to many cultures, including Arabic, Chinese, Babylonian, and many more! This art form that consists of knot-tying and weaving in creative forms to create wall-hangings, bracelets, necklaces, coasters, and other crafty decorations
